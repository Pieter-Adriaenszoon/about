Artisan Italian Cuisine

Italian Cuisine is my speciality, because they use a lot of dairy product and I really love cheese

Pasta are the basic of most Italian dish. Varied by size, shape, and texture. Usually, Pasta are made by combining egg yolk and flour, different type of flour, mix well and stir the dough, kneed carefully and shape the dough, and there you have it, a pasta of your own. I love making pasta, they are just easy to made .

Cheese is also the most important part of Italian dishes. There are Provolone, Mozzarella, Pecorino, Parmegiano, Gorgonzola, and much more. But, due to scarce and price of those Italian genuine cheese. I often substitute them with Dutch cheese. Edam for Parmegiano, and Gouda for Mozzarella, seems heretic, but I promise you won't taste much different.
